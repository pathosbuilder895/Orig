Mathematical Note

The density matrix ρ = Σᵢ wᵢ |φᵢ⟩⟨φᵢ| encodes both the expected feature values and their covariance — feature entanglement — in a single matrix.

Deviation is scored relative to the baseline's self-expectation: 1 − ⟨ξ|ρ|ξ⟩ / ⟨self⟩

This ensures that a same-author submission always scores near 0.0, regardless of the absolute scale of the quantum overlaps.
