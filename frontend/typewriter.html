Typewriter

Writing as thinking. Not writing as producing.

A writing environment stripped of distraction, designed to protect the slow work of thinking through prose — one sentence at a time.

In development

"The sentence is the unit of thought. Get that right and everything else follows."

The idea

A room of one's own for the mind

Most writing tools are production environments. They optimise for output — word count, formatting, sharing, version history. Typewriter optimises for something different: the experience of thinking through writing.

More detail coming as the app takes shape. The work of building it is itself the thing.
