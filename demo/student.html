Formation Opportunity
Voice divergence — worth a conversation
A passage in your recent submission reads differently from your established voice. This is not unusual — it happens to every writer. The formation pathway below gives you a structured way to work through it.
1
Baseline Session
An authenticated writing session in a monitored environment. Establishes your verified voice and gives us a clean reference point for what follows.
2
Formation Session
Targeted writing exercises designed around the dimensions of your voice that diverged. Strengthens your writing where it is developing.
Available after Session 1
3
Verification Session
A new authenticated piece, submitted and scored against your baseline. Successful completion clears the formation flag from your record and closes this pathway.
Available after Session 2
Formation complete. Your record has been updated.
This pathway is developmental throughout. Every step is designed to strengthen your writing, not to assess it. Your tutor is a companion here, not an examiner.
